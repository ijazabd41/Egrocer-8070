# Brand & Style
Coop Discounts is a leading hypermarket in Dubai. The brand revolves around fresh groceries, household products, and unbeatable prices. The design language should be clean, modern, accessible, and retail-focused, driving conversions.

## Colors
- **Primary Red**: High energy, used for Call-to-Actions (Add to Cart, Checkout) and the main header background.
- **Secondary Yellow**: Used for badges, highlights, and promotional accents.
- **Neutrals**: White backgrounds for product cards, off-white for page backgrounds to provide contrast.

## Typography
We are using **Montserrat** for all typography to give a more modern, slightly wider, and friendly geometric look. Sizes are scaled up slightly (+2%) from standard defaults to improve readability across all devices.

## Layout & Components
- **Header**: Contains the Logo, a large central Search Bar, and User Actions (Account, Wishlist, Cart) on the right.
- **Categories**: A grid of rectangular cards (rather than circles) to showcase category images prominently with text overlaid or below.
- **Product Cards**: Clean, bordered cards with the product image taking up the top 60%. Below is the title (2 lines max), price (with old price struck out if discounted), and a prominent "Add to Cart" button.
- **Footer**: A 4-column layout containing Quick Links, Contact Info (including WhatsApp), Categories, and App download links.
